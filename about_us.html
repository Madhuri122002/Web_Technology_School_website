Our Instructional Model

The National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) conducts its courses through its Open Basic Education Programmes (OBE) and Secondary Program.

We focus on skill based learning, that ensures your child acquires the right set of skills, along with academics, that will provide a strong foundation for a fruitful, holistic and meaningful form of education. Vocational, Life Enrichment and community- oriented courses are a part of the curriculum.

Our Vision

To empower students to reach their full potential and become independent and valuable contributers to society.

Creating a world where every child has the opportunity to learn and thrive.

To create a learning environment that puts every child first.

To offer an innovative inclusive approach that nurtures student's intellectual, social, emotional and holistic development.

Regular School

Children will attend offline - regular school. The school will abide to all safety and hygiene protocols as specified.

Ongoing Evaluation Process

Formal assessments procedure based on pen-paper submissions, assignments, quizzes, and discussions. This helps educators evaluate the learning gaps if present and address them accordingly.
